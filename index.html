Bee Game

A simple game that I've made to test some stuffs

The Game

Bee Game is a game based on a test for a job interview application. Initially it was just another test for another job application but then I decided to learn Jasmine and found to be a good opportunity to finish the game

Bees

  • Queen Bee

    • The Queen Bee has a lifespan of 100 Hit Points
    • When the Queen Bee is hit, 8 Hit Points are deducted from her lifespan
    • If/When the Queen Bee has run out of Hit Points, All remaining alive Bees automatically run out of hit points
    • There is onlye 1 Queen Bee

  • Worker Bee

    • Worker Bees have a lifespan of 75 Hit Points
    • When a Worker Bee is hit, 10 Hit Points are deducted from her lifespan
    • There are 5 Worker Bees

  • Drone Bee

    • Drone Bees have a lifespan of 50 Hit Points
    • When a Drone Bee is hit, 12 Hit Points are deducted from her lifespan
    • There are 8 Drone Bees

Gameplay

To play the game you just need to hit the Attack button, one the button is pressed the game will select one bee randomly.

The main objective of the game is to tray kill all the bees but the Queen Bee. If the Queen Bee dies before all the other bees you loose otherwise you win!
